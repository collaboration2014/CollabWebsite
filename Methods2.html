The App

Why we used Collector?

Collector is the only field collection app integrated with the Esri Environment. Other apps, such as QField, are not fully integrated with the Esri environment. Furthermore, many data collection apps are closely tied to database software or require an online connection. Collector allows both offline collection and respects Esri Geodatabase Domains, while allowing data to be collected with either GPS or manually.
